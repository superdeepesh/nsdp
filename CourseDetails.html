Course Title: Software Development Course

Course Objectives/Learning Outcomes: Learn various phases of software development, including how to design, write, test and document computer programs with a variety of tools, using in-demand languages such as Java, C# and SQL and also language skills.

After successful completion of the Programme, the candidate should:

  1. Achieve a broad understanding and working knowledge of the technologies of ICT industry
  2. Intervene professionally at all levels of the software development life cycle.
  3. Develop and write proper documentation for software development projects.
  4. Acquire appropriate communication skills to handle various client facing situations.

Duration: 515 Hours
